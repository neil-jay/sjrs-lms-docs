Welcome to the SJRS Library Management System! This guide is designed for **Students** and **Professors** to help you get started quickly.

## Quick Start

1. **Login**: Access the system using your provided email and password.
2. **Complete Profile**: Ensure your profile information is up-to-date.
3. **Browse Catalog**: Search for books you need.

## Key Features

### **For Students**
* **Borrow Books**: Borrow between **5 to 15 books** (depending on your year of study) for **14 days**.
* **Renewals**: Renew books up to **2 times** online (if not reserved).
* **Reservations**: Reserve books that are currently checked out.
* **History**: View your past borrowing history and reading list.

### **For Professors**
* **Extended Limits**: Borrow up to **10 books** for **30 days**.
* **Renewals**: Renew books up to **3 times**.
* **Priority Access**: Enhanced reservation privileges.

### **For Guests**
* **Basic Access**: Borrow up to **2 books** for **7 days**.
* **Renewals**: Renew books up to **1 time**.

## Common Tasks

### **First Time Login**
1. **Log In**: Use your email and temporary password.
2. **Profile Completion**: You will be prompted to complete your profile details (e.g., Phone Number, Stream). This is **required** to access library features.
3. **Approval**: After completing your profile, your account may require admin approval before you can borrow books.

### **Paying Fines**
If you have overdue books or other penalties:
1. Go to the **"Fines & Penalties"** section in your dashboard.
2. Select the fines you wish to pay.
3. Click **"Pay Now"**.
4. Follow the **UPI** payment instructions (UPI ID or QR code).
5. Enter the **Transaction ID** to confirm the payment.

### **Requesting a Book (Wishlist)**
If a book is not available in the library:
1. Navigate to the **"Wishlist"** or **"Request Book"** section.
2. Click **"Add Request"** or the **"Wishlist"** icon on a book card.
3. Enter the book details (Title, Author, ISBN).
4. Submit the request. You will be notified when the book becomes available.

### **Advanced Borrowing (Request Cart)**
Manage multiple book requests efficiently:
1. **Add to Cart**: Click the **"Add to Cart"** button on books while browsing.
2. **Review Cart**: Open the **Request Cart** drawer (usually an icon in the header) to see your selections.
3. **Bulk Submit**: Click **"Submit Requests"** to request all items in your cart at once.

### **Digital Library & Reading Tracking**
Access e-books and digital resources:
1. **Digital Books**: Access the **"Digital Library"** section to read e-books.
    *   Your **Reading Progress** is automatically tracked (e.g., % completed, time spent).
    *   View your reading stats in the **"My Reading"** tab.
2. **Digital Resources**: Find research papers, past exams, and other files.
    *   Navigate to **"Resources"** or search for specific resource types.
    *   **Download** or **View** files directly in your browser.

### **Academic Tools**
Enhance your research with built-in tools:
* **Citation Generator**: Automatically generate citations in various formats (APA, MLA, etc.) for books and journals.
* **Plagiarism Checker**: Use the experimental heuristic checker to verify the originality of your work.
* **Bookmarks**: Save and organize important sections or books for quick reference.
* **Badges**: Earn badges for your achievements and contributions to the library community.

### **Notifications**
The system keeps you informed via **Email** and **In-App Notifications**:
* **Due Date Reminders**: Sent before books are due.
* **Overdue Alerts**: Sent if a book is late.
* **Reservation Available**: Sent when a reserved book is ready for pickup.
* **Welcome Message**: Sent upon successful registration.

### **Finding a Book**
Use the **Search Bar** at the top of the dashboard. You can search by:
* **Title**
* **Author**
* **ISBN**
* **Genre/Category**

**Note on Reference Materials**:
Some items, like encyclopedias or rare books, are marked as **"Reference Only"**. These cannot be borrowed but can be viewed within the library.


### **Account Security**
Protect your account with advanced security features:
1. **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**: Open **"Two-Factor Authentication"** from your profile menu (or from **Settings**).
    *   Enable **Authenticator App (TOTP)** for time-based codes.
    *   Set up **Email OTP** or **Passkeys (WebAuthn)** as backup methods.
    *   Generate and save **Backup Codes** for emergency access.
2. **Session Management**: Open **"Session Details"** from your profile menu to view active logins and session history.
    *   Check **Device**, **IP**, and **Last Active** time.
    *   Click **"Revoke"** to sign out of suspicious or unused sessions remotely.

### **Personalization**
Customize your library experience:
1. **Notification Preferences**: Go to **"Settings > Notification Preferences"**.
    *   Toggle **Email** and **In-App** alerts for Loans, Orders, and System updates.
    *   Click **"Save Preferences"** to apply changes.
2. **Role Switching**: If you have multiple roles (e.g., Student and Assistant):
    *   Click your **Profile Avatar** in the top right.
    *   Select **"Switch Role"**.
    *   Choose the desired role to change your dashboard view and permissions.

## Academic Tools

Enhance your research and writing with built-in tools.

### **Citation Generator**
Create accurate citations for your bibliography:
1. Navigate to **"Academic Tools > Citation Generator"**.
2. Select the **Source Type** (Book, Journal, Website, etc.).
3. Enter the required details (Title, Author, Year, etc.).
4. Click **"Generate"**.
5. **Copy** the citation to your clipboard or **Save** it to your history for later.

### **Plagiarism Checker (Experimental)**
Check your text for potential originality issues:
1. Navigate to **"Academic Tools > Plagiarism Checker"**.
2. Paste your text into the checker.
3. Adjust the **Sensitivity** if needed.
4. Click **"Check"**.
5. Review the report for potential matches.
    *   *Note: This is a heuristic tool and should not replace professional verification.*

## Journals & Research

Access academic journals and articles directly:
1. Go to **"Journals"** in the main menu.
2. **Browse** by section or **Search** for specific titles.
3. Click a journal to view its **Articles**.
4. Read abstract and details online.

## Community Features

### **Book Reviews**
Share your thoughts on books you've read:
1. Go to a book's detail page.
2. Scroll to the **Reviews** section.
3. Click **"Write a Review"**.
4. Select a **Rating** (1-5 stars) and write your feedback.
5. Submit for moderation.
    *   *Note: Reviews must be approved by a librarian before appearing publicly.*

### **Badges**
Earn recognition for your library activity:
* View your earned badges in **"My Profile > Badges"**.
* Badges are awarded for achievements like "Top Reader", "Punctual Returner", etc.

### **Checking Your Account Status**
Go to **"My Dashboard"** to see:
* **Active Loans**: Books you currently have checked out.
* **Due Dates**: When your books need to be returned.
* **Fines**: Any outstanding fines for overdue items.

### **Renewing a Book**
1. Navigate to **"Active Loans"**.
2. Find the book you want to keep longer.
3. Click the **"Renew"** button.
    *   *Note: You cannot renew a book if another user has reserved it or if you have reached your renewal limit.*
