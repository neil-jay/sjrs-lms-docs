# Renewals & Reservations - FAQ

## Can I extend my loan period?

Yes, you can **renew your book through your account dashboard** if it's not reserved by another user.

**Renewal limits by user type:**
- **Students:** Up to 2 times (each renewal extends by 14 days)
- **Professors:** Up to 3 times (each renewal extends by 30 days)
- **Guests:** 1 time (extends by 7 days)

Each renewal extends the loan period by the **original loan duration**.

## How many times can I renew a book?

Renewal limits depend on your user type:
- **Students:** 2 renewals
- **Professors:** 3 renewals
- **Guests:** 1 renewal

You can only renew if:
- No other user has reserved the book
- You have not exceeded your renewal limit
- Your account has no restrictions due to outstanding fines or violations
- The book is not overdue beyond a certain period

## Can I reserve a book that's currently checked out?

Yes, you can place a **reservation or order request** on books that are currently borrowed by other users.

**Process:**
1. When the book becomes available, your request will be processed
2. You'll be **notified by email**
3. Once approved, the book will be allocated to you
4. Reservations **expire after 48 hours** if not claimed

**Tip:** Check your notifications regularly to stay updated on reservation status.

## Are there restrictions on renewing books?

Yes, you cannot renew a book if:

1. Another user has reserved it
2. You have exceeded your renewal limit
3. Your account has restrictions due to outstanding fines or violations
4. The book is overdue beyond a certain period

## What if I need a book for longer than the standard loan period?

You can renew the book through your account dashboard if it's not reserved. Here are your options:

**Through renewals:**
- Students can renew up to 2 times (extending by 14 days each)
- Professors can renew up to 3 times (extending by 30 days each)
- Guests can renew once (extending by 7 days)

**For longer-term needs:**
Contact the library staff to discuss **special arrangements beyond renewal limits**.

## How do I cancel a reservation or order request?

1. Go to your **account dashboard**
2. Find your **active orders or reservations**
3. **Cancel** any pending request from there

**Important:** 
- Once cancelled, the book will become available for other users
- Note that **approved orders cannot be cancelled** and must be returned through the normal return process

## Can I transfer my borrowed books to another user?

No, borrowed books **cannot be transferred** to another user. Each user must borrow books under their own account.

**If you want to share:** Return it first so the other user can borrow it through their account.
