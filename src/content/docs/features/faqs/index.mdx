## Quick Reference

**Borrowing Limits by User Type:**
| User Type | Books | Duration | Renewals |
|-----------|-------|----------|----------|
| Students (1st Year) | 5 | 14 days | 2 |
| Students (2nd Year) | 10 | 14 days | 2 |
| Students (3rd Year) | 12 | 14 days | 2 |
| Students (4th Year) | 15 | 14 days | 2 |
| Professors | 10 | 30 days | 3 |
| Guests | 2 | 7 days | 1 |

**Late Fees (per day):**
- Students: ₹25/day
- Professors: ₹50/day
- Guests: ₹100/day

**Library Hours:**
- Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Sunday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
