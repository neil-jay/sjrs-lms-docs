# Account Management - FAQ

## How do I create an account?

1. Click on **'Register'** or **'Sign Up'** on the login page
2. Provide your **email address**
3. **Create a password**
4. **Complete your profile information**
5. Receive a **confirmation email** to verify your account

You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account before you can start using the system.

## How do I reset my password?

1. Click on **'Forgot Password'** on the login page
2. Follow the **instructions sent to your email address**

**Important:** Make sure to use the email address associated with your account.

## What should I do if I forget my username?

Your username is typically your **email address**. If you're still having trouble, use the **'Forgot Password'** feature or contact library support for assistance recovering your account.

## What happens if I don't receive my confirmation email?

1. **Check your spam or junk folder first**
2. If you still don't see it, use the **'Resend Confirmation'** option on the login page
3. Make sure you're using the **correct email address** associated with your account

## How do I update my profile information?

1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to your **profile settings**
3. You can update:
   - Personal information
   - Contact details
   - Preferences
4. Save your changes

**Note:** Some changes may require verification.

## How do I get notifications about my account?

Notifications are sent to the **email address associated with your account**. You can manage notification preferences in your account settings to control what types of updates you receive.

## First Time Login

When you log in for the first time:

1. **Log In:** Use your email and temporary password
2. **Profile Completion:** You will be prompted to complete your profile details (e.g., Phone Number, Stream)
   - **This is required** to access library features
3. **Approval:** After completing your profile, your account may require admin approval before you can borrow books

## Can I access library resources from off-campus?

Yes, many digital resources can be accessed remotely through your library account. You may need to:
- Log in with your credentials
- Some resources may require **VPN access** or special authentication

Check the library website for **remote access instructions**.

## What happens to my account if I graduate or leave the institution?

When you graduate or leave, you must:

1. **Return all borrowed books**
2. **Clear any outstanding fines**

Your account may be deactivated after a grace period. **Contact the library before leaving** to ensure your account is properly closed and all materials are returned.

## Are there age restrictions for library access?

Library access is generally available to:
- Registered students
- Faculty
- Staff
- Approved guests

**Specific age restrictions** may apply to certain services or areas. Guest users may need to register and provide identification to access borrowing privileges.
