# Digital Resources & Services - FAQ

## Can I access digital resources?

Yes, the library provides access to various digital resources including:

- E-books
- Academic databases
- Online journals
- Digital collections

These are available through your **account dashboard**.

## Can I access library resources from off-campus?

Yes, many digital resources can be accessed remotely through your library account:

1. **Log in with your credentials**
2. Some resources may require:
   - VPN access
   - Special authentication

**Check the library website** for remote access instructions.

## What is the policy on photocopying and printing?

Photocopying and printing services are available:

- **Charges:** Per page basis
- **Copyright:** You must comply with copyright laws
- **Restrictions:** Some materials may have restrictions on copying

Check with library staff for **current rates and policies**.

## Are there library computers available?

Yes, library computers are available for **academic and research purposes**.

**Policies:**
- Follow the library's **acceptable use policy**
- Respect **time limits** if applicable
- Do not engage in activities that **violate institutional policies or laws**

## What should I do if I need specialized equipment?

Contact the **library staff**:

1. Describe your **specific equipment needs**
2. The library may have:
   - Specialized computers
   - Software
   - Hardware for specific research needs

Availability depends on the library's resources and policies.

## Can I book study rooms or equipment?

Check with the **library about availability**:

1. Some libraries offer **study room reservations**
2. Equipment may be **available for checkout** (cameras, recorders, etc.)
3. Booking procedures vary

Contact the library desk for current options.

## Troubleshooting Remote Access

### I can't access digital resources from off-campus

Try these steps:

1. **Check your login credentials** - Ensure you're using your correct institution ID
2. **Clear browser cache** - Sometimes helps with authentication issues
3. **Try a different browser** - Some resources work better on specific browsers
4. **Use VPN** - If required by your institution
5. **Contact library IT support** - If the issue persists

### I'm having slow download speeds

This could be due to:
- Network congestion during peak hours
- Large file size
- Your internet connection speed

Try:
- Downloading during off-peak hours
- Using a wired connection instead of WiFi
- Contact IT support for assistance

## Can I download books or articles for offline use?

This depends on the specific resource's **license and terms**:

1. Some e-books and articles **allow PDF downloads**
2. Others may have **usage restrictions**
3. Some require **online-only access**

Check each resource's **terms and accessibility options** before using.

## How do I cite digital resources properly?

Different citation styles handle digital resources differently:

1. **MLA, APA, Chicago:** Each has specific formats for digital materials
2. **Library website:** Often provides citation guides
3. **Ask librarians:** Can help with proper citations

The library provides **resources and guidance** on proper citation methods.

## What if I find an error in a digital resource?

If you find incorrect information or technical errors:

1. **Report it to the library**
2. Provide:
   - The specific resource name
   - Details of the error
   - Screenshots if helpful
3. The library will **forward to the provider**

Your feedback helps improve resource quality.

## Are there tutorials for using digital resources?

Yes, the library typically provides:

- **Online tutorials**
- **Webinars**
- **In-person training**
- **Help guides**

Check the library website or ask staff about **available training resources**.

## Can I access the library's digital resources through my phone or tablet?

Yes, many resources are **mobile-friendly**:

1. Log in through your device's browser
2. Some resources have **dedicated apps**
3. Download the library's **mobile app** if available

Check with the library for **mobile access options**.
